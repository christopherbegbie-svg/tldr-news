Understanding Hit-and-Run Laws

Why drivers must stop and report accidents

  • A hit-and-run happens when a driver causes damage then leaves without providing contact info
  • It's illegal in all 50 US states and most countries worldwide
  • Victims can't file insurance claims without driver identification
  • Penalties range from fines to jail time, depending on severity
  • Witnesses and cameras help authorities identify responsible drivers
