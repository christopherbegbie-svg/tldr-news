Minnesota's Prediction Market Ban Explained

Why one state outlawed betting on the future

  • Prediction markets let people bet on future events (elections, weather, sports)
  • Companies like Kalshi and Polymarket operate these platforms globally
  • Minnesota is the first state to make operating them a felony
  • Dozens of other states are also taking legal action
  • Debate centers on gambling risks vs. potential usefulness as forecasting tools
