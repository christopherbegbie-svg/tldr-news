Shark Attacks: Rare But Real

What science reveals about ocean predators

  • Fatal shark attacks are extremely rare—fewer than 10 deaths yearly worldwide
  • Sharks have survived 450 million years by adapting to their environment
  • Most attacks happen when sharks confuse humans with natural prey like seals
  • Great whites can sense one blood drop in an Olympic pool
  • Ocean awareness and safety protocols significantly reduce incident risk
