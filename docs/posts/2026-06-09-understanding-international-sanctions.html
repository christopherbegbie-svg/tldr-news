Understanding International Sanctions

Why countries team up to punish nations economically

  • Sanctions are economic penalties imposed by countries to pressure change
  • They restrict trade, freeze assets, and ban business activities
  • Often used when diplomacy alone isn't working
  • Multiple countries working together makes sanctions more effective
  • They're a tool between peaceful negotiation and military action
