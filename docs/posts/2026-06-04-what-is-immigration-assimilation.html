What Is Immigration Assimilation?

How do we measure integration into a new culture?

  • Assimilation = adopting a new country's language, culture & values
  • It's measured by employment rates, language proficiency, and social integration
  • Research shows assimilation varies by individual, not ethnic group
  • Policy decisions often lack solid evidence backing specific claims
  • Understanding immigration policy helps you think critically about complex issues
