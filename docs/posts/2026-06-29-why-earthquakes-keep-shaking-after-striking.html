Why earthquakes keep shaking after striking

The science of aftershocks explained simply

  • A 4.6 magnitude aftershock struck Venezuela 5 days after twin quakes
  • Over 1,450 people died; tens of thousands remain missing
  • Aftershocks occur when stressed rock around the main quake finally gives way
  • They can continue for days, weeks, or even years after the main event
  • Understanding them helps us build better early warning systems and safer buildings
