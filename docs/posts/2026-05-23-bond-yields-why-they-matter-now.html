Bond Yields: Why They Matter Now

How rising yields impact your loans, jobs, and spending

  • Bond yields measure returns on government and corporate debt
  • Rising yields = higher borrowing costs for everyone
  • When loans cost more, consumers and businesses spend less
  • This slowdown can lead to job losses and lower economic growth
  • Economists use bond yields as an early warning system for economic changes
