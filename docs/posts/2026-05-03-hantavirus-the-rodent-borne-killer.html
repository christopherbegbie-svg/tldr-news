Hantavirus: The Rodent-Borne Killer

Why this rare disease matters for global health awareness

  • Hantavirus is spread by infected rodents through saliva, urine, and droppings
  • Humans contract it by inhaling contaminated particles or direct contact
  • Symptoms include fever, muscle aches, cough, and severe breathing problems
  • It's rare but serious—early detection is critical for survival
  • Three confirmed deaths in recent cruise ship outbreak highlight ongoing risk
