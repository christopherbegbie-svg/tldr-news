States Fight Prediction Market Competition

How technology disrupts government tax revenue streams

  • States like New Hampshire legalized sports betting to fund government services
  • Tax revenue from traditional sportsbooks supports schools, infrastructure, and programs
  • New prediction market platforms (Kalshi, Polymarket) are capturing betting action
  • These platforms operate differently and don't contribute tax revenue the same way
  • States now face pressure to adapt regulations or lose crucial funding
