Guardian Spirits of Ancient Rome

  • Each genius guarded a specific person, household, or location
  • They were believed to bring good fortune and prosperity
  • A rare 1,600-year-old carving was just found in Northern England
  • Such depictions are extremely uncommon in Roman Britain archaeology
