How Governments Lower Fuel Prices

Australia cuts taxes to ease the fuel crisis

  • Australia's states agreed to cut GST on fuel temporarily
  • GST = Goods and Services Tax (like sales tax)
  • Cutting tax = cheaper gas, but less government revenue
  • Trade-off: immediate relief vs. long-term funding for roads, schools
  • Shows how policy directly affects your wallet
