California's Top-Two Primary System

How election rules shape political outcomes

  • California uses a 'top two' primary—all candidates compete together
  • The 2 highest vote-getters advance to November, regardless of party
  • This system was designed to increase competition and choice
  • It can create surprising matchups, like same-party candidates facing off
  • Understanding electoral rules helps you become a more informed voter
