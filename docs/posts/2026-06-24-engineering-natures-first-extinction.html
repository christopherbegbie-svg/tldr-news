Engineering nature's first extinction

Can we genetically erase harmful species?

  • Screwworms are flesh-eating parasites that infect livestock wounds
  • Scientists can now use genetic technology to prevent them from reproducing
  • This would be humanity's first deliberate species extinction
  • Benefits: saves livestock, prevents animal suffering, reduces economic losses
  • Questions: What are the ecological consequences? Where do we draw the line?
