Orangutans Learn to Cross Roads Safely

Conservation engineers build bridges to reconnect fragmented forests

  • Sumatran orangutans are critically endangered—fewer than 14,000 remain
  • Roads fragment forests, trapping animals on isolated patches
  • Conservationists built a canopy bridge high above the Lagan-Pagindar road
  • A young male was filmed using it for the first time—major victory!
  • This proves smart infrastructure can help species survive habitat loss
