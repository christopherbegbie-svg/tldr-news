The Jury System: How Justice Works

Citizens deciding guilt or innocence together

  • Jurors are ordinary citizens who listen to evidence in court
  • Both sides (prosecution & defense) present their arguments
  • Jurors deliberate privately to discuss the facts
  • Verdicts must be based on evidence, not emotion or bias
  • This system prevents any single person from having total power
